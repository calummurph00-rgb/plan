Content formats Daily + long-form

Daily post 1× per day
Short, punchy, one idea. Opinion, observation, or a single framework point. Think: what would you say to a founder in 2 sentences at a conference?
A short take on a misunderstood metric
One thing most Web3 pitch decks get wrong
A data point with a counterintuitive angle
Reply bait — a bold claim founders debate
Long-form thread 1× per week
10–18 tweet thread or a published article. One topic, deeply. Builds authority. Structured like: hook → context → framework → examples → takeaway.
Sector teardown with real data points
Fundraising framework with worked example
Brand/narrative case study (anon if needed)
Ops playbook: how we approach X at Tribe
Research note Bi-weekly
Jynro's zone. 600–1200 words, published as article or long thread. Think Crafts memo but public-facing — fundraising insight, tokenomics pattern, sector flag.
"Why most seed rounds are mispriced in DePIN"
Vesting cliff patterns across 40 rounds
When advisors hurt vs help a raise
Spaces / AMA Phase 2
Once baseline audience is established. Founder interviews, live Q&As around the long-form pieces. Drives follows and shows the team's actual range.
Live breakdown of a real fundraise
Founder AMA: "how we repositioned our brand"
Dealflow data: what we're seeing in market

Growth roadmap Three phases to community

Phase 1 · Now
Foundation
Weeks 1–8 · Build the baseline
  • 1 post/day, 1 long-form/week
  • Establish each team member's voice
  • Hit 500→2k followers milestone
  • Track what resonates by pillar
  • Reply to relevant founders daily
  • Cross-post long-forms to LinkedIn
Phase 2 · When traction shows
Amplification
2k+ followers, consistent engagement
  • Launch weekly Spaces series
  • Founder interview format
  • Introduce co-created threads w/ founders
  • Start building email / newsletter list
  • Pitch journalists/podcasts on threads
  • Paid distribution experiments (small)
Phase 3 · Community signal
Community layer
Real DMs, inbound from founders
  • Open private Discord for founders
  • Monthly research drop (gated)
  • Deal sharing / warm intro network
  • Community becomes a sales channel
  • Annual Tribe Founder Report (public)
